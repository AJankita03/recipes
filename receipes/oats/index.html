Tropical Overnight Oats

delicious overnight soaked oats with yummy fruits.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 8 hrs
  • Preparation: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ¾ cup unsweetened coconut milk beverage
  • ¼ cup diced pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon chopped unsweetened dried mango
  • 1 ½ teaspoons chia seeds
  • 1 ½ teaspoons unsweetened shredded coconut

Instructions

  1. step1: Combine oats, coconut milk, pineapple, mango and chia seeds in a small bowl or jar. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  2. step2: Top with coconut before serving.
  3. step3: TIP: People with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity should use oats that are labeled “gluten-free,” as oats are often cross-contaminated with wheat and barley.
