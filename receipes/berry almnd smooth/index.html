Berry-Almond Smoothie Bowl

A little frozen banana gives creamy texture to this satisfying smoothie bowl.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup frozen raspberries
  • ½ cup frozen sliced banana
  • ½ cup plain unsweetened almond milk
  • 5 tablespoons sliced almonds, divided
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon/li>
  • ⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened coconut flake

Instructions

  1. step1: Blend raspberries, banana, almond milk, 3 tablespoons almonds, cinnamon, cardamom and vanilla in a blender until very smooth. .
  2. step2: Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top with blueberries, the remaining 2 tablespoons almonds and coconut
  3. step3(enjoy): Enoy the tasty recipe.
