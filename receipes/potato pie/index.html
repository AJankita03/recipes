Potato Pie

It is time to dive into a deliciously comforting dish the potato pie. This creation is pure veggie bliss, combining the creamiest mashed potatoes with a rainbow of tender veggies, zesty herbs, and a glorious cheesy topping.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 60 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Potatoes
  • 1/2 cup Peas (shelled)
  • 1 Carrot (small) Cauliflower Florets (a few)
  • 2 tsp Herbs (fresh and minced)
  • 1 Onion (medium, minced) 1/2 tbsp Pepper Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 4 tbsp Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 cup Cheese (grated)
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Ketchup

Instructions

  1. step1: Boil, peel and mash potatoes while still warm with a pinch of salt, pepper powder and 1 tblsp butter.
  2. step2: Chop carrots and cauliflower. Cook with peas and some salt.
  3. step3: Heat oil, fry onions till translucent, add cooked vegetables and stir fry. Add pepper powder, salt, herbs, tomato ketchup and some of the cheese, mix thoroughly, remove from fire. Grease a pie dish thoroughly with some oil, sprinkle 3/4th of the bread crumbs over the entire surface.
  4. step4: On this, spread the potato dough, even out the surface. Top the potato with vegetable mixture, spread evenly. step5: Cover this with the rest of the cheese. Melt the remaining butter, pour over cheese, sprinkle bread crumbs very lightly on this.
  5. step6: Bake in a moderate oven for 20-30 minutes or till top is browned and cheese has melted. Serve hot with soup and buttered bread.
