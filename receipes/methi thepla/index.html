Methi Thepla

Methi Thepla is the most popular Gujarati breakfast dish. Made with fenugreek leaves (methi leaves), it is rich in proteins, fibre, iron and vitamin B6. Methi leaves are mixed with wheat flour and spices, kneaded, rolled out and cooked like a roti.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250 g Wheat Flour
  • 60 g Gram Flour
  • Salt, to taste
  • 5 carrots, coarsely chopped
  • 5 celery stalks, finely chopped

  • Coarsely chopped fresh dill, for serving
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch Methi / Fenugreek Leaves / Vendhaya Keerai approx 200 - 250 g 1 tsp Omam (Ajwain) 1 tsp Garam Masala Powder 1 tsp Turmeric Powder little Sugar as required Curd to knead the dough as required Oil as per taste Salt
